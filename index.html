Meet Dr. Fallert

Your Recovery Starts with the Right Therapist

Dr. Kristen Fallert is a Doctor of Physical Therapy who has been serving patients across the St. Louis metro area for over 12 years. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Exercise and Movement Science from Missouri State University and completed her Doctorate in Physical Therapy at Long Island University-Brooklyn in 2013.

She specializes in orthopedic physical therapy with advanced training in manual therapy techniques. Her Orthopedic Manual Therapy Certification (COMT) and expertise in Instrument Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization (IASTM) allow her to deliver hands-on care that gets to the root of your pain, not just the symptoms.

Dr. Fallert is passionate about helping active adults stay healthy, move better, and perform at their best. At Align Physical Therapy, every session is truly one-on-one. No interruptions. No double-booking. Just you and your therapist focused entirely on your recovery.

Areas of Expertise

Specialized Orthopedic Treatment

Dr. Fallert brings over a decade of focused experience treating these conditions for patients across Kirkwood, Webster Groves, Des Peres, and the greater St. Louis area.

TMJ Dysfunction

Jaw pain, clicking, locking, and limited opening treated with specialized manual therapy techniques that address the root cause of TMJ disorders.

Cervicogenic Headaches

Headaches originating from the neck and upper spine respond extremely well to targeted manual therapy and corrective exercise programs.

Neck Pain & Cervicalgia

Chronic and acute neck pain, stiffness, and cervicalgia treated through evidence-based manual therapy, postural correction, and strengthening.

From IT band syndrome to plantar fasciitis, Dr. Fallert helps runners recover from injury and build the strength and mechanics to stay on the road.

Do I need a referral for physical therapy?

No referral is needed. Missouri allows direct access to physical therapy, which means you can schedule your initial evaluation at Align Physical Therapy without a doctor's referral. Just call (314) 630-2403 or email Kfallert@AlignPTstl.com to book.

Does Align Physical Therapy accept insurance?

Align Physical Therapy is a cash-based practice and does not bill insurance directly. This model allows for longer, more focused sessions. HSA cards are accepted, and superbills can be provided for you to submit to your insurance for potential out-of-network reimbursement.

How much does physical therapy cost?

Initial evaluations are $200 for a 75-minute one-on-one session. Follow-up visits are $175 for 60 minutes. Package discounts are available: 5 sessions at $170 each ($850 total) and 10 sessions at $160 each ($1,600 total).

What conditions do you treat?

Dr. Fallert specializes in TMJ dysfunction (jaw pain), cervicogenic headaches, cervicalgia (neck pain), and running injuries. She also treats back pain, shoulder injuries, joint dysfunction, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

What are your clinic hours?

Monday 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Wednesday and Friday 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The schedule can be flexible, so reach out if you need an appointment before or after regular hours.

Where is Align Physical Therapy located?

Align Physical Therapy is located inside Elevate Salon Suites at 10505 Big Bend BLVD, Suite C Unit 2, Kirkwood, MO 63112. Conveniently located for patients from Kirkwood, Webster Groves, Des Peres, Glendale, Sunset Hills, and the surrounding St. Louis area.

What is cash-based physical therapy?

Cash-based physical therapy means you pay directly for your care instead of going through insurance. This allows for longer one-on-one sessions, personalized treatment plans, no referral requirements, no double-booking, and faster results with fewer total visits.
